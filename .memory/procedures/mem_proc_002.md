To invoke a skill: (1) open `.github/skills/<skill-name>/SKILL.md`, (2) follow its process step by step. To change a skill, open a PR in `davidamitchell/Skills` — never edit `.github/skills/` directly. After the PR merges, advance the submodule pointer here with `git submodule update --remote .github/skills`.
